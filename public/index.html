Schedule

The schedule is easier to understand at a glance.

This is the core value of the product. Instead of forcing instructors to interpret a clunky portal view, WhisSchedule presents the day in a format that is fast, readable, and useful in the moment.

Why this matters

The app makes the daily workload easier to read, especially when a day includes multiple assignments or private details. It reduces friction and helps instructors stay focused on teaching, not on decoding the schedule.

Stats

The season becomes easier to understand and manage.

WhisSchedule goes beyond schedule viewing. It gives instructors a clear picture of workload, busiest periods, lesson mix, and overall season rhythm, which adds real value over simply displaying rows of data.

Pay

Pay visibility makes the app more useful week to week.

The pay view helps instructors connect scheduled hours to likely earnings across the season. That makes the app practical, not just informative, and gives people another reason to rely on it regularly.

Onboarding

The product already feels ready for broader use.

The onboarding flow makes the experience approachable from the first launch. It helps position WhisSchedule as a product that could be adopted more broadly rather than a niche internal tool.

WhisSchedule is a clear, useful improvement to the instructor experience. It takes information people already depend on and turns it into something faster, more usable, and far easier to act on.
